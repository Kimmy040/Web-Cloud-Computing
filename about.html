About WhatToWatch

WhatToWatch is a website that allows you to search for movies and TV shows. Not only can you search for movies and TV shows, but you can also add them to your favorites list. You can also shuffle a random movie or TV show .

How to use WhatToWatch

To use WhatToWatch, simply type in the name of the movie or TV show you want to search for in the search bar. Then, click the "Go" button to search for the movie or TV show. If you want to add the movie or TV show to your favorites list, click icon. If you want to shuffle a random movie or TV show, click the "Shuffle" button.
