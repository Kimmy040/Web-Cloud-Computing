About Watchmode

Watchmode is a website that allows you to search for movies and TV shows. Not only can you search for movies and TV shows, but you can also add them to your favourites list. You can also shuffle a random movie or TV show.

How to use Watchmode

To use Watchmode, simply type in the name of the movie or TV show you want to search for in the search bar. Then, click the "Go" button to search for the movie or TV show. If you want to add the movie or TV show to your favourites list, click the "Add to favourites" button. If you want to shuffle a random movie or TV show, click the "Shuffle" button.
